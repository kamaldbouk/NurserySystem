Curriculum

Subject Description
Language Development Storytime, vocabulary building, and conversation skills
Mathematics Basic counting, sorting, and identifying shapes and colors
Science Introduction to the natural world through sensory play and simple experiments
Arts and Crafts Exploration of different materials, colors, and techniques to encourage creativity
Music and Movement Singing, dancing, and playing with musical instruments
Physical Education Development of fine and gross motor skills through play and structured activities
Social Studies Introduction to different cultures, traditions, and community helpers
Emotional Development Building self-awareness, confidence, and empathy through stories, role-playing, and group activities
